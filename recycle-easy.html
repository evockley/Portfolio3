Recycle Easy

Machine Learning and AI

Overview

Our planet faces a crisis of pollution and waste that’s led us to a tipping point. 300 million tons of plastic are produced each year and half of it is discarded after a single use.

We created an app that intelligetly guides users towards better, more effective waste disposal choices. It’s now or never.

Tools

Research

Surveys

We wanted to target the right audience with our research ultimately our app. So we created a survey on Google Forms and shared it as widely as possible via social media.

51 total individuals responded. 55% of respondents were Millenials, and 64% of them live in urban communities. We chose to focus on urban Millennials for an MVP.

Interviews

We conducted 5 user interviews that fit our target audience. We found that users generally believe that recycling is a good habit to have. Confusing informational websites and changing recycling practices present fundamental challenges to knowing if items are recyclable. Sometimes users toss things in the recycling without knowing if it’s actually recyclable.

Ideate

What if...

We imagined a wide variety of solutions including:

  • Uber for recycling
  • Getting recommendations for good recycling places
  • Take a photo of your item and let the app figure it out

Prioritize

Based on our reserach, we knew we would have to make this as easy as possible while also keeping it informative. We created a 2x2 matrix of our best ideas and felt the photo feature would be the easiest for users to learn what to do with their items.
