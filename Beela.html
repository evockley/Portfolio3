with client goals

Phase 1 research uncovered a lot of user needs. In order to do the most effective and useful work possible, we began Phase 2 with an outcomes workshop.

Why we did this: Focusing our efforts on one business goal allowed us to target our user-centered research and design efforts on activities that would support the most important business problem and focus on outcomes (increased revenue) rather than outputs (a new homepage).

Approach

We met the Beela Stakeholders in Miro and asked them to individually create stickies with:

  1. Their priorities for Beela
  2. Their most important business outcomes

Next, we asked them to connect their priorities to the business outcome those priorities would drive.

Then, we did a quick grouping activities, labeled the groups, and asked them to prioritize the labeled groups in order of importance

The stakeholders prioritized REVENUE.
